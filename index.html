Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What's the difference between packages? +

Basic ($50) gives you a beautiful 2-3 minute song with AI-enhanced vocals and professional mixing. Premium ($150) includes a professional vocalist, 3 revisions, behind-the-scenes video, acoustic version, and personal video message. Both include Spotify/Apple Music release, but I retain royalties to keep prices affordable.

Who owns the rights to the song? +

With Basic and Premium packages, I retain copyright and streaming royalties, while you get unlimited personal use rights. You can listen, share, and gift the song freely. For commercial use or full ownership, add Rights Transfer for $500 to get complete copyright, master recordings, and all future royalties.

How long does delivery take? +

Basic packages are delivered within 48 hours. Premium packages take 5-7 business days due to additional production elements. The song appears on streaming platforms 7-10 days after completion. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Can I use the song commercially? +

Basic and Premium packages are for personal use only. For commercial use (ads, videos, business), you need either a Sync License ($200-1000 for single project) or Rights Transfer ($500 for complete ownership). Wedding Package includes ceremony usage rights.

What if I want changes to my song? +

Basic includes 1 revision, Premium includes 3 revisions. Additional revisions are $20 each. I work closely with you to ensure your song perfectly captures your vision. Most clients are thrilled with the first version!

How does the streaming release work? +

I handle all technical aspects of releasing your song on Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, YouTube Music, and 150+ other platforms. You'll receive links to share once it's live. The song stays online forever - unlike an MP3, it's always accessible to anyone you share it with.
